SDG 2: Zero Hunger

SDG 2 seeks to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture. It focuses on ensuring everyone has access to sufficient and nutritious food year-round, eradicating all forms of malnutrition, and doubling the productivity and incomes of small-scale food producers. The goal also emphasizes sustainable food systems and the preservation of genetic diversity in crops and livestock.
